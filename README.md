Puskar AI – Your Smart Email & Chat Assistant
Puskar AI is a powerful, mobile-friendly AI assistant designed to help users generate professional emails, reply intelligently, and interact through a smooth chat interface. Built with OpenAI’s API, it provides:
Email Generation: Instantly create polished, professional emails from short prompts.
Smart Email Replies: Craft perfect responses to incoming messages, saving time and improving communication.
Interactive Chat: Conversational AI that understands context and responds naturally.
Mobile-Friendly Design: Fully responsive layout for phones, tablets, and desktops.
Smooth Animations & UI: Modern, user-friendly interface with chat bubble animations and smooth scrolling.
Customizable: Adaptable for personal, business, or educational purposes.
Puskar AI is the perfect digital assistant for anyone who wants to save time, improve communication, and experience AI-powered productivity.
